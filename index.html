    Computer Wizards Club

    "Compiling curiosity into reality, one line of code at a time."

    Why Join Us?

    We believe in empowering students through digital exploration. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, we provide a collaborative space to grow your technical skills.

    • Advocating for digital literacy and open-source contribution
    • Building a network of tech enthusiasts
    • Access to exclusive software licenses and tools

    What We Have to Offer

    • Skill Development: Weekly coding bootcamps covering Python, web dev, and game design.
    • Hackathons: Bi-annual 24-hour coding challenges with prizes.
    • Industry Talks: Guest speakers from leading tech companies.
    • Project Incubation: Turn your app ideas into functional prototypes with team support.

    Knights Yearbook

    "Preserving today's moments for tomorrow's memories."

    Why Join Us?

    Be the historian of our university. Joining the Knights Yearbook means you are entrusted with capturing the essence of the student body, events, and milestones.

    • Gain real-world publication experience
    • Build a professional portfolio in media
    • Free access to premium photography gear

    What We Have to Offer

    • Photography: Master studio lighting, portrait, and event photography.
    • Journalism: Write compelling copy and conduct interviews with seniors.
    • Graphic Design: Learn Adobe InDesign and layout principles.
    • Press Passes: Exclusive access to front-row seats at major school events.

    Red Cross Youth

    "Humanity, Impartiality, Neutrality, Independence, Voluntary Service."

    Why Join Us?

    Make a tangible difference in people's lives. We train students to be first responders and compassionate community leaders.

    • Be part of a globally recognized humanitarian network
    • Earn official certifications in life-saving skills
    • Develop leadership and crisis management abilities

    What We Have to Offer

    • First Aid Training: Comprehensive CPR and basic life support courses.
    • Blood Drives: Organize and assist in campus-wide blood donation events.
    • Disaster Relief: Participate in packing and distribution during calamities.
    • Health Campaigns: Advocate for hygiene and mental health awareness.
